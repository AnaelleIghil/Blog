# Livre 

Livre, livre toi à moi. 

Livre : assemblage de papiers imprimé et réuni en volume. D'après le Larousse. 

Humain : assemblage de souvenirs, d'histoires dont on ne se souvient pas forcément. Ma définition. 

Rencontre : fait de rencontrer quelqu'un, de se trouver en sa présence sans l'avoir cherché. D'après le Larousse. 

Rencontre : événement qui devait se produire entre deux personnes. Ma définition. 

J'ai toujours aimé faire un parallèle entre les romans et les humains. Après tout, quand on rencontre quelqu'un, on devient part de son histoire, parfois on est une virgule, parfois un chapitre entier. 

Books : assembly of printed paper, jointed together in volume. Larousse definition. 

Human : assembly of memories, stories, know and unknown. My definition. 

Encounter : act of meeting someone, without looking for it. Larousse. 

Encounter : event that needed to happen between two people. My definition. 

I always love to connect books and human. After all, when you meet someone, you became a comma, a sentence or even more a chapter in them history. 

I wasn't looking to meet anyone at that time Freddie, but you appeared. And changed the way I see life. But we are two different books, on two different part of the bookshelf. I don't explain our connection, I don't want to. But you're unique to me. A pause in italics in my own book. I hope one day I'll be able to write a whole chapter about the day we'll finally meet in person. But even if it's just a sentence, it would be enough. Cause all these part in italics keep me alive. I just hope I'll never see our final point.
